After you see the value and choose your minimum outside option in each round, the computer will randomly draw twenty numbers between 0 and 300. This will represent twenty possibly outcomes you could have seen in this round.

  • For each draw, any number is equally likely to be drawn.
  • A draw below your minimum will result you choosing to Invest. You will receive the value.
  • If a draw is at or above your minimum, you will not Invest. You will receive the outside option.
