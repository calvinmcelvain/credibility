As an investor, your best choice is always to set the slider exact equal to the value you believe you will earn on average by choosing Invest.

To help you understand why this is true, you will now complete a short training exercise.

  • There are 5 rounds in the training exercise.
  • In each round, the computer will randomly select a value that you will earn by choosing Invest.
    • You will know the randomly selected value.
    • You will earn this value for certain if you choose Invest.
  • In each round, you will use a slider to select the minimum outside option for which you will choose not to invest.

After you see the value and choose your minimum outside option in each round, the computer will randomly draw twenty numbers between 1 and 50.

  • A draw below your minimum will result in you choosing to Invest. You will receive the value.
  • If a draw is at or above your minimum, you will not Invest. You will receive the outside option.

Our advice is that you should always set the slider exactly equal to the value. This will always earn you at least as much money as you would have earned by choosing something else.
