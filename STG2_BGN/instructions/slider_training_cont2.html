After each round, you will receive feedback. This will show you the twenty randomly drawn outside options. For each outside option, the feedback will show you four things:

  • The value of the outside option.
  • What you earn given the minimum outside option you picked with the slider
  • What you would have earned if you had set the minimum outside option equal to the value
  • The difference between these two numbers

As an investor, your best choice is always to set the slider exactly equal to the value.

The feedback will highlight in red all outside options where you would have earned more by setting the slider equal to the value than the minimum outside option you chose.

The feedback will highlight in green all outside options where you would have earned less by setting the slider equal to the value than the minimum outside option you chose.

One outside option will randomly be selected for payment. Your payoff is highlighted in yellow.
