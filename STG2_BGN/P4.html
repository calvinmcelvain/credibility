In Decision 1, There are two possible qualities of the investment opportunity, Low or High, with each quality being equally likely. Player A sees the true Quality and then sends all Player Bs a recommendation (“Invest” or “Do Not Invest”). All Player Bs receive the same recommendation and then simultaneously choose whether or not to invest.

Player A will receive 20 for each Player B who chooses to invest. This is shown in the payoff table below.
