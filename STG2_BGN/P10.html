Decision 3 has several similar features to Decisions 1 and 2. There are two possible qualities of the investment opportunity, Low or High, with each quality being equally likely. Player A sees the true Quality and then sends all Player Bs a recommendation (“Invest” or “Do Not Invest”). All Player Bs receive the same recommendation and then simultaneously choose whether or not to invest.

As in Decision 2, Player A will receive 100 if the majority (three or more) of Player Bs choose to invest, regardless of Quality. This is shown in the payoff table below.
