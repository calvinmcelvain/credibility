As an Investor, your endowment will be randomly drawn from the numbers between 1 and 400. There was a separate draw for each Investor in Scenario 1, so knowing your endowment in either Scenario 1 or Scenario does not tell you anything about the endowments of other Investors. As an Investor, you will use a slider to select the minimum endowment for which you will choose Keep if the Advisor’s advice in Scenario 1 was Invest. Your best choice as an Investor is always to set the slider exactly equal to the average amount you believe you will earn by choosing Invest. The average amount you will earn as an Investor if you choose Invest is the probability that you will win the prize multiplied by 400. Here again, the probability of winning the prize is your perceived probability that the majority of other Investors in your group choose Invest in Scenario 1.
