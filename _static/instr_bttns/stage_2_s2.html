As in Scenario 1, each Investor’s endowment will be randomly drawn from the numbers between 1 and 400. There is a separate draw for each Investor, so knowing your endowment does not tell you anything about the endowments of other Investors. As an Investor, you will select the minimum endowment for which you will choose Keep . Your best choice as an Investor is always to set the minimum endowment exactly equal to the average amount you believe you will earn by choosing Invest. The average amount you will earn as an Investor if you choose Invest is the probability that you will win the prize multiplied by 400, where the probability of winning the prize is how likely you believe that the majority of other Investors in your group choose Invest if the Advisor’s advice is Invest, regardless of Quality.
