Scenario 1

Scenario 1 involves different payoff tables than Stage 1 for both the Advisors and Investors.

In Scenario 1, as in all scenarios, the Advisor will receive 100 ECU for each Investor who chooses to invest. This is shown in the payoff table below. The Advisor’s payoffs does not depend on the quality of the investment opportunity.

As an Advisor, you will receive a signal telling you the quality of the investment opportunity. That signal will always be correct. You will select what advice to send the Investors for each possible signal (Low or High).

Each Investor’s endowment will be randomly drawn from the numbers between 1 and 400. There is a separate draw for each Investor, so knowing your endowment does not tell you anything about the endowments of other Investors. As an Investor, you will select the minimum endowment for which you will choose Keep if the Advisor’s advice is Invest. Your best choice as an Investor is always to set the minimum endowment exactly equal to the average amount you believe you will earn by choosing Invest. The average amount you will earn as an Investor if you choose Invest is the probability that you will win the prize multiplied by 400, where the probability of winning the prize is how likely you believe that Quality is High if the Advisor’s advice is Invest.
