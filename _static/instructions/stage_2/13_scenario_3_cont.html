Investors who choose Invest will win the 400 ECU prize if the majority of Investors in their group (three or more) choose Invest and Quality is High. Unlike Scenario 2, the payoff of an Investor depends on the quality of the investment opportunity.

As in Scenarios 1 and 2, each Investor’s endowment will be randomly drawn from the numbers between 1 and 400. There is a separate draw for each Investor, so knowing your endowment does not tell you anything about the endowments of other Investors. As an Investor, you will use a slider to select the minimum endowment for which you will choose Keep if the Advisor’s advice is Invest. Your best choice as an Investor is always to set the slider exactly equal to the average amount you believe you will earn by choosing Invest. The average amount you will earn as an Investor if you choose Invest is the probability that you will win the prize multiplied by 400..
