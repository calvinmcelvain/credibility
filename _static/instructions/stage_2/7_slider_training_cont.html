After each round, you will receive feedback. This will show you the twenty randomly drawn endowments. For each endowment, the feedback will show you four things:

  • The endowment
  • What you earned given the minimum endowment you chose using the slider (”Your Choice”)
  • What you would have earned if you had set the minimum endowment equal to the value of investing as we advise (“Our Advice”)
  • The difference between what you would have earned from following our advice and what you earned from your choice.

As an investor, your best choice is always to set the slider exactly equal to the value of investing.

The feedback will highlight in green all cases where you earned more from your choice than if you had followed our advice.

The feedback will highlight in red all cases where you earned less from your choice than if you had followed our advice.
