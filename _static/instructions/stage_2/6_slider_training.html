As an Investor, your best choice is always to set the slider exactly equal to the value you believe you will earn on average by choosing Invest.

To help you understand why this is true, you will now complete a short training exercise.

  • There are 5 rounds in the training exercise. The first two rounds are for practice and will not be paid. The last three rounds will be paid.
  • In each round, there will be a value of investing. This is the amount that you will earn for certain by choosing Invest.
    • You will know the randomly selected value of investing.
    • You will earn the value of investing for certain if you choose Invest.
  • In each round, you will use a slider to select the minimum endowment for which you will choose Keep.
