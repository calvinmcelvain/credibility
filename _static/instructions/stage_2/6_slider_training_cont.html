After each round, you will receive feedback. This will show you the twenty randomly drawn outside options. For each outside option, the feedback will show you four things:

  • The value of the outside option.
  • What you earned given the minimum outside option you chose using the slider (”Your Choice”)
  • What you would have earned if you had set the minimum outside option equal to the value as we advise (“Our Advice”)
  • The difference between what you would have earned from following our advice and what you earned from your choice.

As an investor, your best choice is always to set the slider exactly equal to the value.

The feedback will highlight in green all cases where you earned more from your choice than if you had followed our advice.

The feedback will highlight in red all cases where you earned less from your choice than if you had followed our advice.
