This experiment will consist of two stages. We will go through the instructions for Stage 1 now, and you will receive instructions for Stage 2 later.

Stage 1 will last for 30 rounds. In each round, you will be in a five-person group with four other participants. The participants you are grouped with will be the same for all rounds of Stage 1. Your group will consist of participants in two roles. Four of the participants will participate in the experiment in the role of Investors. The remaining participant in each group will be in the role of Advisor.

You will be informed of your role (Advisor or Investor) prior to the beginning of Stage 1.
