You will use an interface like the one shown on the screen to choose the minimum endowment for which you will select Keep. You can choose a minimum endowment three ways: moving the slider, entering a minimum endowment into the text box labeled “minimum endowment”, or entering a probability of winning the prize into the text box labeled “probability”.
